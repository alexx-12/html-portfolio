The Best Movies According to Alex

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

How To Make Millions Before Grandma Dies

How to Make Millions Before Grandma Dies is a heartfelt and comedic film that explores the quirky bond between generations. I love this movie because it reminds me of my grandma, who has always taken care of me with love and wisdom, making every moment feel special—just like the characters in the story.

Lolo and the Kid

Lolo and the Kidis a touching story about the bond between a grandfather and his grandchild, filled with warmth and life lessons. I like this movie because it reminds me of my own grandfather, who has always been a source of love, guidance, and unforgettable memories.

Inside Out

Inside Out is a beautifully animated film that takes viewers on a journey through the emotions inside a young girl’s mind. I like this movie because it’s not only fun and creative, but it also shows how our feelings work together to shape who we are.
